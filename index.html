Real Learning:

Whether it be inside or outside the classroom setting, real learning takes place when there is a motivational incentive (intrinsic or extrinsic) that keeps the learner engaged with material that is appropriate for their current understanding of the subject (not too difficult that it causes frustration, not to easy that it causes boredom).
